Introduction

Where Devotion
Meets Community

Welcome to the Arusha Satsang Mandal website. Under the guidance of Pujya Mahant Swami Maharaj, BAPS Shri Swaminarayan Mandir in Arusha, Tanzania is a stunning Hindu temple. We believe in living a life of devotion, service, and self-improvement, fostering spiritual harmony and ethical conduct.

BAPS Temple Arusha

A stunning Hindu temple built with intricate carvings, peaceful atmosphere, and a variety of facilities, providing a unique, spiritually enriching experience.

Nilkanth Varni

Nilkanth Varni inspired us to live a life of devotion, service, and self-improvement. Let us embody his virtues of compassion, kindness, and humility.

Akshar Purushottam Darshan

A Hindu philosophy that emphasizes devotion to God, ethical conduct, and pursuit of self-realization, unlocking transformative power in daily life.
